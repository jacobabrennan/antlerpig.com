About

AntlerPig is the personal website of amateur game developer Jacob A. Brennan, and home to his various web projects. The blog is updated twice a week, with a release of some form on Sunday, and a progress and planning report on Wednesday.

What's with the animal thing?

An Antler Pig is a fictitious ungulate mammal and the subject of many drawings. The site may eventually host a webcomic by the same name. Meanwhile, an Antler Pig serves as the mascot for my projects and the site in general.
